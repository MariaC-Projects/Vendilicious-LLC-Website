Our Story

Convenience you can taste.

At Vendilicious, we prioritize quality, convenience, and customer satisfaction. Our state-of-the-art machines are placed where people need them most.

We started with a single vending machine and a passion for mouthwatering snacks. Today, we deliver tasty convenience across the Bay with seamless setups and reliable upkeep.

Our team specializes in placements, stocking, and maintenance that keep service uninterrupted and customers happy.
